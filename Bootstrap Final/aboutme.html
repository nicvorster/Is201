Who is Nic Vorster?

I am just a South African who loves his country!

I was born in Durban, South Africa, and lived there until I was 18 years old. I grew up going to the beach, playing sports with friends and playing in the streets of Westville.

My family wouold frequently visit the amazing Kruger National Park, where I developed a great appreciation for wildlife and the power they possess. There is nothing more humbling than seeing a lion up close, feeling the fear but also the amazement of these creature's stature.

Whether it's the warm Durban waters, or the cool Cape Town beaches, they both offer two totally different experiences. I recommend going to both, seeing the Zulu culture near the Durban beach and the penguins and wildlife in Cape Town. South Africa has such a variety of cultures and life experiences to see.
